In simple terms, Undertale: Funk Mode is yet another Undertale FNF mod in a long line of them that aims to remain as faithful as possible to the source material while making the most of everything Undertale has to offer.

 

The story will be largely the same as the original Undertale, but with slight changes to give it a fresh feel and to adapt it for BF and GF's inclusion.

 

Additionally, we’re adding small, unique elements to make the gameplay a bit more entertaining for the player!

 

There really isn’t much surprise here—it’s just Undertale, but what if, ermmm, BF and GF were part of that universe and Frisk didn’t exist?

 

We have many plans and ideas for this project, since in some ways we want it to be a "love letter" to the Undertale community, if you want to see it that way. Still, projects like this are very time-consuming, so we’ll be working as efficiently as possible.
